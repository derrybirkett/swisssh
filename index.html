The Love Shot

Each shot is "icon-ed" by the ratio of likes to views. The more love Dribbblers show for a shot compared to its view-count determines the ratio.

Hey, those icons are so lame, when you gonna beef 'em up?

Hey, the icons for each shot change sometimes - what gives?

The shot ratio is recalculated everytime - so if the ratio changes (less or more likes per views) so do the icons.
